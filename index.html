Engineering Summit

One Liner about the event

ABOUT

The importance of engineers in our society cannot be understated. The contributions they make to building the society with the tools at their disposal is something that is often understated.

Engineering Summit, a national level summit, is a conference to teach participants what it takes to become a real engineer working in the industry. With prominent personalities in their respective fields, this summit is sure to enlighten you on the varied prospects of engineering.

There will be a general track followed by a multiple track session and finally a mentoring session, where participants get to interact with top minds from many different fields.

General Track

Hear the top minds in the society talk about how important the field really is in this modern day and age from their multitude of experiences. To those who hold doubts about the role engineering plays in shaping our future, this session will open your eyes to the great possibilities that lie ahead.

This is a golden opportunity to gain insight from the brightest minds in the field of engineering. For our speakers will remove all doubts and will voice their opinions on just how integral engineering is for a progressive future. As a result, Engineering Summit hopes to invoke the engineer in you and help you prepare to be part of a positive change in the future of humanity.

Parallel Tracks

Listen to the most relevant and insightful lectures in Computer Science, Nuclear Energy and Biotechnology. Pick what piques your interest and learn more about the varied areas your stream has to offer.

Icon

The Future of Consumption (Track 1)

The future of retail is personalisation. Listen to this enthralling talk about how our future in retail will be invisible and omnipresent, with simpler transactions and no storefronts, which will make the act of buying a seamless part of contemporary life.
Icon

Biotechnology (track 2)

Learn more about the emerging and re-emerging virus infections and the role of accurate diagnoses for disease control or genomics and stem cell technology, the scope and future of Regenerative Medicine. Equip yourself to research ,design and develop innovative products and processes that could benefit the health sector.
Icon

Nuclear Energy (track 3)

For decades, atomic energy has been shrouded in a deep mist of misconceptions, fueled by Soviet-era horror films. Learn about how nuclear energy embodies the saying, "Small is beautiful" and how nuclear energy is the way towards a greener future.
